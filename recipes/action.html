Act Now!

Description

Doing, no matter how small, is the only real step towards making anything happen

Ingredients

  • Courage
  • Failure
  • Resilience

Instructions

  1. Accept failure as a chance to learn, never as the end.
  2. Have courage to act knowing it might fail, otherwise you will stand still forever.
  3. Do not be insane, you cannot expect change if you are doing the same thing.
