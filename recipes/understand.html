Understanding

Description

Understanding trumps anything that tries to stand in the way of your Liberty.

Ingredients

  • Patience
  • Pain
  • Perspective

Instructions

  1. Find a problem and use trial and error to come up with possible solutions
  2. Be willing to be patient and endure pain, as it is most likely required to truly understand.
  3. Be open minded, take on a growth mindset, and be comfortable working with different perspectives.
